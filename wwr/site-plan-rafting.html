Overview

Purpose

Rivers rafting is a natural enviroment place so families, friends and acquaintances can spent their time and meet new people. The adventures they will live will be unique and being one with nature, numerous adrenaline-infused rapids with just enough tranquil floating . Our team is destined to introduce them the best experience in an exciting trip mastering the rivers through the most beautiful sceneries that nature can offer as great hiking, fishing & swimming and are perfect for families. Live a wildlife where they can see bears, eagles, deer and all kinds of wildlife. We also have a camping area where they can light fires and tell their best stories. We have the most affordable prices and all the equipment to make their whitewater rafting trip one of the most extreme and safe. We invite them to explore our website to learn more about our tour guides, our various activities and that 40 years of experience supports us by giving them the trip of their dreams, they can also get in touch with us through our numbers and media social, we invite them to know our promotional packages with unique discounts that you will have at their disposal. We look forward to catching their attention so they can live the next adventure

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on) we want our clients to be of all ages, to create a family area where they can enjoy different activities, target those people who want to spend a vacation time and also those who like extreme life, open for school excursions and gastronomic companies to be able to establish their commercial establishments What do they want? What they want is to get out of a working life in the city for a moment and enjoy the landscapes, also those who like to venture and live moments full of adrenaline What are their needs that aren't being met? What they are looking for is to go to a place where they can have many unique experiences, a place to entertain themselves and also to spend some time. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? My audience generally enters by cell phone because the greatest promotion will be done through social media based on those searches related to extreme sports or places to vacation with their families or friends, It will also be an audience that enters through other devices such as laptop, it will even be a good place for those people retired from their jobs
