How to protect your kitty

We share the main cares to be aware for kittens so they can grow up happy and without difficulties. First you must identify if the cat is pregnant, a growth in the volume of the cat's body is notable. When it is first time it can be a little doubtful if it is really pregnant, however there is a sign to which we must be very aware and prepared in the behavior of the cat, since it will begin to look for hiding places or get into the clothes looking for a new place where accommodate if your site is not the most comfortable. It is important that as soon as we notice this behavior we provide her with a comfortable place and out of danger, and even more important is that the cat DOES NOT GET STRESSED.

Find the kittens

if you cat desapear or is not at home constantly, try to find where she had her babies, you should do this as soon as posible so you can see if the new kittens are in good conditions, if they are not, put them into a box without touch, try to use a cloth without scents detergents

Newly Born

When your kittens are newborns, don't look at them, let alone touch them. At this moment, the mother cat is stressed by the birth and her instincts are to protect her babies.
